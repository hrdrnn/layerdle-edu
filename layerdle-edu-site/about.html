What is Layerdle?

Layerdle is a browser‑based puzzle that challenges your musical ear. Each puzzle presents five audio stems—from drums through vocals—which are gradually revealed over five rounds. Your task is to identify the song title from the layers alone. It’s inspired by the simplicity of Wordle but tailored for music education and discovery.

Education Edition

This Education Edition sources its stems from publicly available multitrack projects, notably the Cambridge‑MT Mixing Secrets library and the Slakh2100 dataset. These recordings are provided under educational or Creative Commons licences. We group each project’s tracks into five canonical categories—drums, bass, chords, melody and vocals—to create a consistent guessing experience.

Why play Layerdle?

Recognising songs from isolated parts is a useful skill for composers, performers and producers. By focusing on individual instruments, you’ll start to notice how each layer contributes to the overall texture. It’s also a fun way to discover new music and appreciate lesser‑known artists whose work might not appear on the radio.
