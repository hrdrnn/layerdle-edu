Your Data & Privacy

Layerdle respects your privacy. This site does not collect personal information. The audio files (stems) are fetched directly from the URLs you provide in your manifest and are played locally in your browser using the Web Audio API. They are not uploaded or stored on our servers.

To improve performance, a service worker caches the site’s static assets (HTML, CSS, JavaScript and icons) so that it can work offline. Audio files are not cached by the service worker; they are streamed fresh each time you load a puzzle.

Please ensure that you have the right to use any stems you host and link via your manifest. We do not assume responsibility for copyright infringement.
