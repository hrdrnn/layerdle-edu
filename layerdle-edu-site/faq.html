How do I play?

Enter the URL of a manifest.json that lists the day’s puzzle along with links to the five stems. Tap Load Today’s Puzzle and then initialise audio once. Round 1 will play only one instrument. Each subsequent round adds another layer. After listening, type your guess in the box and submit. You have six guesses in total.

What are stems?

A stem is an isolated part of a song (for example, drums or vocals). Professional recordings are often multitracked, and educational projects like Cambridge‑MT make these tracks available for mixing practice. We group them into five broad categories for simplicity.

Where do the songs come from?

The Education Edition uses stems from projects released under educational or Creative Commons licences. See the About page for details. You can also customise the manifest to point to your own recordings if you have permission to use them.

What does Hard Mode do?

Hard Mode randomises the order of instrument layers and reduces the number of plays per round to one. It also withholds the text clue until you submit an incorrect guess in the final round.

Can I share my result?

Yes! After you finish the puzzle, a share button appears. It copies a summary of your attempt—including a simple progress grid—to your clipboard. You can paste this into social media or messaging apps.
